Meet Klaviyo UX Designer Ally Hangartner

I’m a thinker. I’m an observer. I’m a designer @ Klaviyo.
At Klaviyo, an email marketing platform geared towards eCommerce companies, we’re building software that businesses rely on. They use Klaviyo as the primary way to talk to their customers and their “source of truth” for customer information. Design @ Klaviyo plays a huge part in making complicated data interactions simple. We strive to minimize the time our customers spend building and analyzing a marketing pipeline and instead let them focus on what they do best; sell awesome products.
