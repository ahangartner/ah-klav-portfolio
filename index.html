List Imports

Maintaining data integrity is integral to creating effective data-driven strategies. That is why Klaviyo built a comprehensive list import experience that allows customers to control, effectively and consistently, what data enters their account.

Account User Management

Adding new users to your Klaviyo account is paramount to enabling growing teams to work fast and smart. In revamping the user management tools we added two features, user roles and permissions and multi-account users. Now it is super easy to add new users to your account with specific permissions as well as move fluidly between your different Klaviyo accounts.

Dynamic Coupons

Adding coupons to email campaigns is shown to be an effective marketing strategy to engage customers. Recognizing this, Klaviyo has created an easy way to create Shopify coupon codes ahead of time so customers email campaigns can be sent in a timely and effective manner all from the Klaviyo app.

Klaviyo Redesign

The Klaviyo UX team has undertaken work to redesign the Klaviyo app to be more modern and usable for customers. Work will include updating the current UI to a more flexible, modular experience as well as building a design system that will serve as the backbone for all future features.
